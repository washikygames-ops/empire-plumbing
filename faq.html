How quickly can you get to me?
For standard jobs we aim to be with you the same day or next business day. For emergencies — burst pipes, gas leaks, sewage backups — we treat those as urgent and get there as fast as we can, including nights and weekends.
Do you give quotes before starting work?
Always. For large jobs — bathrooms, renovations, new builds — we provide a written quote before a single thing is touched. For smaller call-out work, we diagnose and tell you the cost before we start. No surprises on the invoice.
Are you licensed and registered?
Yes. We are fully licensed for plumbing, gas fitting, and drain laying work. We are also IQP registered for backflow prevention testing in both the Manawatu District Council and Palmerston North City Council areas.
What areas do you cover?
We're based in Feilding and cover the entire Manawatu region — Palmerston North, Marton, Woodville, Dannevirke, and surrounding areas. Have a job a bit further out? Give us a call. We're willing to travel for the right job.
Do you do commercial and industrial work?
Yes. We work across residential, commercial, and industrial. Restaurants, apartment blocks, retail, offices — we handle it all. Backflow prevention testing is also available for commercial properties requiring IQP certification.
Is there a call-out fee?
Standard hourly rates apply from when we arrive. After-hours callouts have a 2-hour minimum charge of $150/hr. We'll tell you exactly what to expect before we come out — no nasty surprises.
