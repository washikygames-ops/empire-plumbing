Who We Are

Not a big faceless company.
Just good tradespeople.

Empire Plumbing has been serving the Manawatu since 2015 — built on showing up, being honest, and doing the job right.

Est. 2015 · Feilding, NZ

Empire Plumbing was started in December 2015 by two tradespeople who were sick of seeing the same story — customers left waiting, jobs half done, inflated invoices. We built something different.

George and Mark have been doing this work for years. They show up on time, explain what needs doing in plain English, and don't leave until the job is right. That's why most of our work comes from referrals — not advertising.
